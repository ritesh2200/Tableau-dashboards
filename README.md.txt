 Sales Analytics Dashboard for Superstore USA

This Tableau dashboard analyzes sales performance for Superstore USA, providing insights into product categories, regional sales trends, customer behavior, and profitability.

 Features
- **Sales Performance**: Tracks total sales, profits, and the number of orders by category and sub-category.
- **Regional Sales Trends**: Visualizes sales distribution and profitability across various regions in the USA.
- **Customer Behavior**: Analyzes customer purchase patterns and identifies key customer segments.
- **Product Profitability**: Highlights the most and least profitable products.

 Tools Used
- Tableau
- Excel
